`PokeQuest` is a reference web app for finding `Pokemon Quest` information without jumping between scattered tables or guides.

The goal is to make recipes, attraction rates, moves, and Pokedex data quick to explore from a clean responsive interface.

[Open PokeQuest](https://pokequest.opeshm.net/){target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}

## What it offers

- Recipe search with ingredients, quality, and the Pokemon each recipe can attract.
- Moves section with type, power, wait time, and compatible Pokemon.
- Pokedex with profiles, evolutions, obtaining methods, and related recipes.
- Navigation designed for desktop and mobile.

## Why I built it

`Pokemon Quest` has useful information spread across different sources. This app centralizes that data into a more direct experience for planning recipes and checking details while playing.
