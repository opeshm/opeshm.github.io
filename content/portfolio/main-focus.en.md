`Main Focus` is a simple Android app designed to keep your current main goal visible.

The core idea is direct: choose your `Main Focus`, define the roadmap to achieve it, and keep it present on your phone's home screen through a widget.

## What it does

- Lets you define one clear main goal.
- Lets you break it down into roadmap tasks or steps.
- Shows the goal status directly in a widget.
- Congratulates you in the widget when every task is complete.

## Why it is useful

Many productivity apps become too heavy. `Main Focus` takes the opposite approach: a lightweight interface and one important idea visible all the time.
