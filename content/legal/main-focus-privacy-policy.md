# Privacy Policy for Main Focus

Last updated: 16 April 2026

`Main Focus` is an Android application designed to help users define a main goal, manage a roadmap of tasks, and display progress through a home screen widget.

## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may process and how that information is used.

## Information Collected

`Main Focus` is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly.

The app stores the information you enter in order to provide its functionality, such as:

- Your main focus text.
- Your roadmap or task list.
- The completion status of your tasks.
- Widget-related state needed to display your progress.

## Personal Data

`Main Focus` does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location unless you explicitly provide it outside of the app.

## How Data Is Used

The information entered into the app is used only to:

- Display your main focus.
- Manage your roadmap and task completion state.
- Update the widget shown on your device.
- Show a completion message when all tasks are done.

## Data Sharing

`Main Focus` does not sell your data.

`Main Focus` does not share your app data with third parties for advertising purposes.

## Data Storage

App data is stored locally on your device unless a future version explicitly introduces cloud or online features.

## Internet Access

If a version of the application does not require network features, then the app does not need to send your content to external servers in order to perform its main functionality.

## Children’s Privacy

`Main Focus` is not directed specifically at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Security

Reasonable care is taken to keep the app simple and limit unnecessary data handling. However, no method of electronic storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updated version will be published at the same page URL.
